CHRIS JACOBS DESIGN
VIRTUAL INTERFACE DESIGN / DEVELOPMENT / IDENTITY
Chris Jacobs is a User Experience Designer (digital and physical) and Creative Consultant living in Los Angeles. His work has spanned many disciplines from Packaging and Industrial Design to Architecture and User-Interface Design.
More about Chris
Chris has worked in Los Angeles, Bombay, Hong Kong, Beijing, and Singapore. He co-founded the Interactive company United Future (Culver City) in 2006, staying until 2011. He currently has a select group of clients he contracts with in his own design consultancy, Chris Jacobs Design. He's married to the phenomenal photographer and commercial actress, BeBe Jacobs.
