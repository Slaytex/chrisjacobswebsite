Chris Jacobs is a User Experience Designer and Creative Consultant living in Los Angeles. He's been hard at work in this field for just over 18 years. He's a graduate of Art Center College of Design.
Some accomplishments
Chris has founded two design companies, and sold his first to a publicly traded company. He's worked in Los Angeles, Bombay, Hong Kong, Beijing, and Singapore. He was responsible for the interaction and user-interface design for Interpublic Group Emerging Media Lab's latest "Glass touch screen" technology for the retail industry, which is IPG's flagship touch-point product on display in the Los Angeles and New York offices. He co-founded the Interactive company United Future (Culver City) in 2006, and left in 2011 to pursue new adventures. He currently works as Interactive Creative Director at Tytan Advertising in Tybee Island, Georgia. He's Chief Innovation Officer at Demand Magic in Los Angeles, and has a select group of clients he contracts with in his own design consultancy, Chris Jacobs Design. He's married to the phenomenal photographer and commercial actress, BeBe Jacobs, who is the mother of two beautiful boys. He currently resides in Los Angeles.
